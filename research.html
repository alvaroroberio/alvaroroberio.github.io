Research

The works and research are concentrated in the area of Quantitative Methods in Economics, working mainly in Regional, Agricultural, and Climate Economics.

My specific research areas include:

  • Economic impact of climate change in the semiarid region
  • Extreme weather events and economic development
  • Applied topics in health economics, education and crime
