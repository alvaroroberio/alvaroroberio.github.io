Applied Economist. PhD student in Applied Economics

Federal University of Juíz de Fora - UFJF

I am an applied economist and registered with Corecon (PE) n° 5278. PhD student in Applied Economics (2022-2026) at the Federal University of Juíz de Fora (UFJF), Minas Gerais, Brazil. Currently, developing research under the supervision of Profa. Dra. Danyelle Branco (UFPE).

I am also a journal reviewer: Caderno de Saúde Coletiva (UFRJ), Análise Econômica (UFRGS) e Planejamento de Políticas Públicas (IPEA).

I work primarily on Regional Economics, Agricultural and Climate. My research interests span:

  • Economic impact of climate change in the semiarid region
  • Extreme weather events and economic development
  • Applied topics in health economics, education and crime
